Privacy & Data Handling

Privacy Policy

This page explains how Appolyze handles storage access, advertising services, and user privacy when you use the application.

Last updated: August 2025

Quick Summary

Appolyze ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our application handles information when you use our mobile application. By using Appolyze, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

All file analysis and storage scanning operations are performed locally on your device.
Your files never leave your device and are not transmitted to our servers.
No files are automatically removed without user confirmation.

1. Information We Collect

Appolyze does not collect or store personal information such as name, email address, phone number, contacts, photos, files, or location data. All file analysis and storage scanning operations are performed locally on your device. Your files never leave your device and are not transmitted to any server.

2. Storage Access Permission

Appolyze requires access to device storage in order to provide core functionality such as scanning storage usage, identifying duplicate photos, detecting similar photos, finding blurred photos, identifying large files, reviewing downloads and documents, and cleaning unnecessary files. This permission is used only to analyze and display files to the user. Appolyze does not collect, upload, or share any file data. All processing happens locally on the user's device.

3. Advertising

Appolyze uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect certain information automatically to provide personalized or contextual ads, including device identifiers, advertising ID, approximate location, and app interaction data. This data collection is handled by Google and is subject to Google’s privacy policies.

For more information, please visit Google Privacy Policy.

4. Third-Party Services

The application may use third-party services including Google AdMob for advertising. These services may collect information used to identify you for advertising or analytics purposes. Appolyze does not control how third-party services process data.

5. Data Security

Appolyze does not store personal data on external servers. All file analysis is performed locally on your device, which helps minimize privacy risks.

6. Children’s Privacy

Appolyze is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the application, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

7. User Control

Users have full control over their files. The application only deletes files when the user explicitly selects them. No files are automatically removed without user confirmation.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Users will be notified of significant changes through the app or the Play Store listing.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@appolyze.com.
