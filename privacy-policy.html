Privacy Policy

This page explains how Appolyze handles storage access, permissions, advertising services and user privacy when you use the application.

Last updated: April 2026

Quick Summary

Appolyze ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. The application is designed with a privacy-first architecture.

All file scanning and storage analysis is performed locally on your device.
Your files and contacts never leave your device.
No files or contacts are removed without user confirmation.

1. Information We Process

Appolyze does not collect, store, or transmit personal information such as name, email address, phone number, photos, files, or contacts.

Some information on your device may be temporarily processed locally in order to provide features such as storage analysis, duplicate photo detection, duplicate contacts detection, and file management.

All processing happens entirely on the user's device. No personal information is transmitted to Appolyze servers.

2. Storage Access Permission

Appolyze requires storage access permission to analyze device storage and provide cleaning features such as:

• Storage usage analysis • Duplicate photo detection • Similar photo detection • Blurry photo detection • Large file discovery • Download and document review

These operations are performed locally on the device. Appolyze does not upload, store, or transmit file data.

3. Contacts Access Permission

Appolyze may request permission to access contacts on your device in order to provide the Duplicate Contacts Cleaner feature.

This feature analyzes the address book on your device to detect duplicate or repeated contact entries.

All contact processing is performed locally on the device. Contact data is never transmitted, stored, or shared with external servers.

Users always maintain full control and must confirm any modification or removal of contacts.

4. Permissions Used by the Application

Appolyze only requests permissions that are necessary to provide specific features of the application.

Storage Permission

Required to scan device storage and analyze files such as photos, videos, downloads, documents and large files.

Contacts Permission

Used exclusively for detecting duplicate contacts in the user's address book.

If users choose not to grant permissions, some features may not function correctly.

5. Advertising

Appolyze uses Google AdMob to display advertisements within the application.

AdMob may automatically collect certain information including device identifiers, advertising ID, approximate location, and application interaction data.

This data collection is handled by Google and is governed by Google's privacy policies.

For more information please visit: Google Privacy Policy

6. Third-Party Services

Appolyze may use trusted third-party services including:

• Google AdMob (advertising) • Google Play Billing (subscription payments)

These services may collect information required to provide their functionality. Appolyze does not control how third-party services process data.

7. Data Security

Appolyze does not store personal data on external servers. All analysis and processing occurs locally on the user's device, which significantly reduces privacy risks.

8. Children's Privacy

Appolyze is not directed toward children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. User Control

Users have full control over their files and contacts. The application never deletes or modifies data automatically. All actions require explicit user confirmation.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Users will be notified of significant changes through the application or the Play Store listing.
